Can I have multiple accounts?

You only need one personal account with MultiCurrencyWallet — even if you have multiple addresses or multiple bank accounts. As long as the bank account that we receive your money from is held in your own name, you can send transfers from any currency that we have available.

What if I've forgotten my password?

If you're logged out and you forget your password, you'll need to reset it. To do this:

  1. Click the Reset password button below Reset Password
  2. Enter your email address and click the Send password reset link
  3. Open your email and click on the reset password link
  4. Follow the prompts and enter the requested information
  5. Set your new password

What can I do with the MultiCurrencyWallet app?

Using them, you can send money abroad from anywhere with internet. You can check the status of your transfers and, if need be, cancel them.
Our apps have all the information you need to make a bank transfer to us, whether you're online or at your local branch. You can also make debit or credit card payments using the app.
