How Does Our Payment System Works?

Our main target is to led currency-exchange market around the world. That’s why We organized local accounts for specific currencies for users.

So, for example, if you want to convert your (£) pounds to (€) euros, you send the money in pounds to Multi-Currency Wallet’s (£) Pound account. The equivalent amount is then sent from Multi-Currency Wallet’s(€) euro account to the recipient.

Tips for getting started with MultiCurrencyWallet

MultiCurrencyWallet is an online account that lets you send money, get paid, and spend money internationally. With a MCW account, you can send money abroad, get paid in other currencies, and spend abroad on the MCW debit Mastercard.
Here are some tips to help you get started.

Get verified to open your multi-currency account

Creating a multi-currency account with MultiCurrencyWallet unlocks the full set of features available to our customers. This means you’ll be able to:
  1. hold 3+ currencies in balances in your account
  2. convert currencies instantly in your account with low fees
  3. get bank details to receive AUD, EUR, GBP, PLN, and USD with no fees
  4. set up Direct Debits in INR and EUR
  5. order a MultiCurrencyWallet debit card (currently available in Europe, US, Australia, and India).
  6. spend on the debit card in valid currency with low fees.

We use the real, mid-market exchange rate
Banks and other providers often set their own, unfriendly exchange rates. It means that you pay more than you need to, and they pocket the difference.
At MultiCurrencyWallet, we never mark up the exchange rate. You’ll always get the real mid-market exchange rate — like you’d find on independent sources like Google, XE, Yahoo Finance or Reuters.

Can I have multiple accounts?

You only need one personal account with MultiCurrencyWallet — even if you have multiple addresses or multiple bank accounts. As long as the bank account that we receive your money from is held in your own name, you can send transfers from any currency that we have available.

What's a guaranteed rate?

A guaranteed rate is exactly what you’d expect: an exchange rate that's guaranteed for a specified period of time. We offer guaranteed rates for all of our transfers.
As you finish setting up a transfer, we'll guarantee your rate for a certain period of time. And as long as we receive your money within that period, you’ll get that rate locked. If your money doesn't reach us in time, we'll still convert and send it as soon as possible. But we'll use the mid-market rate instead.
